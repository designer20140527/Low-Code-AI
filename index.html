Low-Code AI Creation

Low-Code AI simplifies the AI development process with a user-friendly graphical interface. Users can easily build AI models by dragging and dropping components, selecting data sources, setting training goals, and defining output methods. No coding knowledge is required, making AI accessible to everyone, from beginners to experts.

AI Model Training & Deployment

Low-Code AI offers decentralized model management, where all AI model versions are stored securely on the blockchain. Users can participate in decentralized training by contributing data or computational resources. Once training is complete, models are deployed using Web3 protocols, ensuring decentralized delivery and reducing risks associated with centralized infrastructure.

Decentralized AI Model Marketplace

The platform features a decentralized marketplace where users can list, buy, or rent AI models. Every transaction is securely recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and security. Users can access a wide range of pre-trained models or share their own, creating a dynamic, peer-driven ecosystem.

Incentive & Reward System

Low-Code AI integrates a robust incentive mechanism to encourage active participation. Users can earn $LCAI tokens by contributing data, computational resources, or AI models to the platform. Token rewards are also distributed for successful model trades in the marketplace, incentivizing creators to list and share their models. This decentralized incentive structure ensures that every contribution is valued, promoting a vibrant, dynamic ecosystem where both developers and data providers are rewarded for their engagement.
